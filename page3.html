Centaur -
The Noblest Epyllion in Modern

A handmade leather book by me about “Centaur”, “the noblest and gentlest typeface” in the world. It aims at introducing a complete conspectus of the Centaur typeface. Illustration, publication and research done by the author.

Research & Development

Typographic Anatomy

As there are similarities and differences between Jenson and Centaur, there is a typographic research on basic typographic anatomy.

Research of pattern and color

After conducting research on Centaur, patterns of that period of the Bible is found and developed. A color scheme is set based on the color used by the Holy Bible.

Illustration

In order to further express the sincere sacred feeling of the typeface, more floral patterns are designed.
