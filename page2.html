Concept & Intention

Yiu Fung Pickled Fruits for Pregnant is a new premium box packaging for pregnant with blessings and medical values highlighted. The idea is that the outer box indicates mother and the inner translucent packaging indicates baby. The color scheme and form are set according to the characteristics of the pickled fruits itself. As suggested by “Compendium of Materia Medica”,  ( 本草綱目 ) dried plums, ginger, licorice menthol and tangerine peel can make one salivate so as to prevent morning sickness of the mother-to-be. The latest packaging design also aims to enhance user experience and aesthetics. Therefore, hot foil stamping technique is applied to present the preciousness and greatness of the pregnant. With golden foil stamping on the outer straw mat surface, the value of the premium box is also presented.

Process & Development

Identity

Design the new brand identity for the new edition.
Tried to figure out the colour for presenting pregnant, softness, protection and preciousness. The target audience is the middle-class pregnant. The shape of each box is designed according to the structure of the pickled fruits chosen.

3D Rendering 

Using Adobe Dimension to draft out the outer appearance of the packaging and test the arrangement of the four boxes.

Inner Structure

Since pickled fruits are not an official medicine, most of the people neglect their medical values and this is reported in the newspaper. Hence, the medical values are highlighted on the surface of the inner box. With golden foil stamping on the outer straw mat surface, the value of the premium box is also presented. This kind of cross-over of materials (paper and straw mat) is also fresh to the consumers.

Dummy making with the use of hot golden foil stamping and material testing.
