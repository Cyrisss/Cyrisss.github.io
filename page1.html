Grave of the Fireflies

Movie title logotype design

Concept

This is a Chinese movie title redesign of the war animation "Grave of the Fireflies" directed by Isao Takahata (original novel written by Nosaka Akiyuki). The concept of the latest version is combining the symbolic meaning from fireflies (soul). Also, the colors and abstract appearance of fire indicate war. At the same time, the Chinese character is different for “ 蟲 ” so as to present the fighter crafts in the sky during World War II, which synchronizes the older and subtle version in the original poster published.
