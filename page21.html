*Okiku

Japanese Yokai
Experimental Typography

Three Japanese Yokai namely Zashikiwarashi, Okiku and Rokurokubi are selected based on the book “Night Parade of One Hundred Demons” (百鬼夜行 )to design a typeface experimentally.

Story behind

Okiku accidentally broke the master’s family treasure of ten plates in a set (one said that the master hid it deliberately), and then she was killed by the owner and thrown into the well. Since then, Okiku ‘s voice of "one...two...three" sorrowfully counts the plates every night from the bottom of the well, and when he counts to the ninth, he starts to cry, and then counts from the beginning.
