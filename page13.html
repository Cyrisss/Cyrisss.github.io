The Funeral of Corals

Photogram (594*841 mm, @130*130 mm)

Intention

Photogram is a medium to present the shadows and light of the objects and witness the elapsing of time. My photograms recorded shocking moments I have experienced in Okinawa where I saw a beach filled with dead corals. The photograms show how corals turn white when human causes ice melting in this irreversible 13 x 13 tragedy to our nature with Manzhushahua.
