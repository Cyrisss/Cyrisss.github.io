*Zashikiwarashi

Japanese Yokai
Experimental Typography

Three Japanese Yokai namely Zashikiwarashi, Okiku and Rokurokubi are selected based on the book “Night Parade of One Hundred Demons” (百鬼夜行 )to design a typeface experimentally.

Story behind

The presence of Zashikiwarashi originated the spirits of children who were crushed to death and buried at the home in the Tohoku region. The infanticide was called Usugoro ("mortar kill") in order to reduce the number children for lower the expenses for each family. After killing, they were buried in the dirt floor room (Doma) or in the kitchen. The spirits of the children would walk around outer edges as told.
